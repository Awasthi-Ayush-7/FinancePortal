About FinanceX

"Beware of little expenses; a small leak will sink a great ship" - Benjamin Franklin
A simple finance application to make your life easier.

Our Application

The application's goal is to provide a one-stop financial platform for controlling your costs by tracking your expenses.

This portal allows you to link your bank account and conduct transactions with other accounts in order to settle your balances.

The portal also has tools to help you manage your spending by offering a concise summary of your monthly costs and classifying them.
